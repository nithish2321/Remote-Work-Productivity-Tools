Frequently Asked Questions

How do I integrate the tool with Slack?
To integrate with Slack, go to the Integrations page and click on "Slack." Follow the prompts to authorize the connection.
How can I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with a reset link. Follow the instructions to set a new password.
What platforms are supported?
Our tools support integrations with Slack, Zoom, Google Drive, Microsoft Teams, Asana, and Trello. More platforms are being added regularly.
